Chicken Yakisoba

Recipe

A traditional Japanese noodle recipe. A hearty meal made with chicken and cabbage.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp canola oil
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 2 skinless, boneless chicken breast cut into pieces
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp Asian-style chile paste
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp canola oil
  • 1/2 medium head cabbage, thinly sliced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 carrots, cut into matchsticks
  • 1 tbsp salt
  • 2 lb. cooked yakisoba noodles
  • 2 tbsp pickled ginger (optional)

Steps

  1. Step 1 - Heat 1 tbsp canola oil and 1 tbsp sesame oil in larget skillet in medium-high heat.
  2. Step 2 - Add chicken and garlic into oil and cook until fragrant (about 1 minute)
  3. Step 3 - Add chile paste into chicken; cook and stir until chicken completely browned (~3-4 minutes)
  4. Step 4 - Add soy sauce and simmer for 2 minutes. Pour into a bowl once finished.
  5. Step 5 - Heat 1 tbsp canola oil in skillet over med-high heat; cook and stir cabbage, onion, carrots, salt until cabbage is wilted (~3-4 minutes)
  6. Step 6 - Add chicken into vegetables and stir. Add noodles and cook until chicken is no longer pick inside. Garnish with pickled ginger.
